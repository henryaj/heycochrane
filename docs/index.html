  • Does Vitamin C help with colds?

    Partly, yes.

    Regular supplementation seems to shorten how long your cold is, but it doesn't make you less likely to get sick in the first place.

  • Does paracetamol help with lower back pain?

    No.

  • Should I wear a bicycle helmet?

    Yes.

    Wearing a helmet dramatically reduces the risk of injury to your head and face if you're in an accident.

  • Do antidepressants work better than placebo for treating depression?

    Yes.

    SSRI and tricyclic antidepressants work better than placebo when treating depression.

  • Should people take statins preventatively (even if they haven't been diagnosed with heart disease)?

    Probably.

    Even in patients without evidence of cardiovascular disease, statins reduce all-cause mortality without causing any serious harm.

  • Is cannabis (marijuana) helpful for neuropathic pain?

    No.

    There is a lack of good evidence that any cannabis-derived product works for any chronic neuropathic pain.
